  • Football

    The Virginia Cavaliers football team represents the University of Virginia in the sport of American football. They are the current Belk Bowl Champions. The Cavaliers compete in the Football Bowl Subdivision of the National Collegiate Athletic Association and the Coastal Division of the Atlantic Coast Conference.

  • Baseball

    The Virginia Cavaliers baseball team represents the University of Virginia in NCAA Division I college baseball. They were the 2015 College World Series Champions. Established in 1889, the team participates in the Coastal division of the Atlantic Coast Conference and plays its home games at Davenport Field. The team's head coach is Brian O'Connor.
