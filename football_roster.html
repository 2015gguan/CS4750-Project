3
Bryce Perkins

In 2018 Perkins was one of two players in the nation with 2,600+ passing yards (2,680) and 900+ rushing yards (923), joining Heisman Trophy winner Kyler Murray (Oklahoma) ... finished No. 1 in the ACC and No. 13 in the nation with 206 points responsible for ... also was No. 3 in the ACC and No. 21 in the nation with 3,603 yards of total offense ... set the UVA single-season record for total offense with 3,603 yards ... set the UVA single-season record with 34 touchdowns responsible for ... tied the UVA single-season record for 100-yard rushing games by a quarterback with four ... his completion percentage of 64.5 percent ranks No. 3 all-time on UVA's single-season ledger ... his 25 passing touchdowns are tied with Kurt Benkert (2017) for No. 2 all-time on UVA's single-season list ... the 25 passing touchdowns also put him in a tie for No. 2 in the ACC ... started all 13 of UVA's games ... he also became the first Cavalier to rush for 100+ yards in each of his first two career games ... rushed for 61 yards against No. 16 Miami ... also passed for 92 yards ... was 20-of-32 passing for 189 yards and one touchdown at Duke ... also rushed 15 times for 61 yards and two more scores against the Blue Devils ... was 21-of-26 passing for 217 yards and one touchdown at Georgia Tech ... also rushed 16 times for 73 yards and another score against the Yellow Jackets ... was 14-of-29 passing for 259 yards and three touchdowns at Virginia Tech ... also rushed 24 times for 112 yards against the Hokies ...was 22-of-31 passing for 208 yards and three touchdowns against South Carolina in the Belk Bowl.
